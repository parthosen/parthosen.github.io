About me

Hi! I’m Partho Sen .

I am passionate about Data Science.I work with biological and clinical data.

I develop and apply practices in computational biology by using mathematical modeling and machine learning techniques on x-omics (transcriptomics, metabolomics, metagenomics, etc.) datasets to study autoimmune and metabolic disease / disorders such as Type 1 Diabetes (T1D), Celiac disease, Obesity and Nonalcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) in humans.
